Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Nonprofit Management independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

  • NonprofitReady.org Learning Library – Over 500 free on-demand courses, certificates, videos, and guides covering all aspects of nonprofit work.
  • Philanthropy University Full Course Catalog – Free global nonprofit training initiative with courses on leadership, fundraising, strategy, and more.
  • Harvard Kennedy School Nonprofit Webinars and Resources – Free webinars and executive education insights on financial stewardship and leadership.
