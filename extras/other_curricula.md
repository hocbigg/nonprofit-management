# Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Nonprofit Management independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

- [NonprofitReady.org Learning Library](https://www.nonprofitready.org/) – Over 500 free on-demand courses, certificates, videos, and guides covering all aspects of nonprofit work.
- [Philanthropy University Full Course Catalog](https://courses.philanthropyu.org/courses) – Free global nonprofit training initiative with courses on leadership, fundraising, strategy, and more.
- [Harvard Kennedy School Nonprofit Webinars and Resources](https://pll.harvard.edu/subject/nonprofit-management) – Free webinars and executive education insights on financial stewardship and leadership.
